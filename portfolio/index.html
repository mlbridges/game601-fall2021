My games from GAME 601 - Game Prototyping at American University in Fall 2021

Game Modification: Matching Pennies
This game modification attempts to improve on the simple game of flipping a coin. Players compete to acquire points by flipping a coin either into their hand or into a box; after a minute, the person with the highest score wins.

Hypertext Fiction: The Key
This interactive fiction game follows a person through a repeating day cycle where everything seems to go wrong. When they find a key on the ground, it may be the key to their salvation (or the harbinger of chaos...)

Interactive Fiction: The House
This piece of interactive fiction uses Inform 7 to tell the story of a person who is trapped in a deeply unsettling house. Pursued by a suit of armor, they must find the key to the front door and make it out of the house before it's too late.
PLAY
Programming with p5js
This p5 digital art piece uses arrays and loops to create a series of hot air balloons and clouds, which peacefully float across the screen. The hot air balloons and clouds have their respective hue and size adjusted based on their position in their array.

(Final) Digital Game: Hot Air Balloon Adventure
This final digital game allows you to play as a hot air balloon soaring through the sky. Dodge obstacles and avoid lightning strikes with the help of your friends in order to make it to the hot air balloon festival in one piece!
